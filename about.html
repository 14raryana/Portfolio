About

My name is Raman Aryana, and I have recently started taking Case Western Reserves coding bootcamp that they offer. I graduated from John Carroll University in 2019 with a bachelors in biology. From childhood I had always been interested in computer programming, and once I finished my biology degree I realized that programming is not only the career path that best suits me, but also what I truly enjoy doing. I heard about bootcamps and that they were on the rise in popularity and I figured that this was an extremely great opportunity to be able to get my foot in the door in the world of programming.
